Battle Reference

Space Combat

Pre-combat abilities are executed in defender's choice of order, except: PDS before Space Mines; AFB before Sabotage Run.

  1. Pre-combat abilities
  2. Announce Withdrawals/Retreats
  3. Roll All Combat Dice
  4. Remove Casualties
  5. Execute Announced Withdrawal/Retreat
  6. Repeat Steps 2-4

Invasion Combat

For multi-planet systems, attacker must declare troop division ahead of time.

  1. Bombardment (Dreadnought if no PDS; War Sun)
  2. PDS Fire
  3. Roll All Combat Dice
  4. Remove Casualties
  5. Repeat Steps 3-4
